Inovation

Today, the core of computer networks is, when compared with servers, desktops, and mobile devices, an unfriendly environment for innovation. Among the factors that contribute to such hostile environment for innovation are:
  • necessity of deep and global modifications in order to adopt new solutions
  • slow standardization process required to grant interoperability with legacy protocols
  • dependency on the profit-oriented interest of network equipment vendors
  • tendency of network core devices on using closed software and proprietary hardware, ruling out any possibility of customization from end-users.

ProViNet

ProViNet relies on the concepts of Software-Defined Networking and Network Virtualization Environment to meet the requirements of ease, fast, and flexible deployment of new network services.
